# BA-V2.0-D Store AI Operation

Status: PASS

## Daily Operation

1. Core Enterprise Data Layer supplies SAP-derived sales, inventory, product, customer, task, and event facts.
2. Store AI generates daily briefings before store opening.
3. Store managers review health, risks, opportunities, and AI-created tasks.
4. Employees use WeCom AI for product, sales, customer, and operation questions.
5. CEO reviews Huyan Store Intelligence Overview for all-store health, top risks, top opportunities, and AI recommendations.
6. Execution feedback is captured for AI Memory.

## Guardrails

No SAP logic modification, no duplicate business database, human approval for execution-impacting recommendations, and scoped access for all users.
