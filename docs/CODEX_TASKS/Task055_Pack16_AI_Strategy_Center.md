# Task055 - Pack 16 AI Strategy Center

## Objective

Build the AI Strategy Center for strategic planning, OKR alignment, scenario comparison,
expansion analysis and explainable strategic recommendations.

## Completed

- Added OKR service linked to unified KPIs.
- Added strategy model service.
- Added multi-scenario comparison.
- Added expansion planning analysis.
- Added brand and product strategy analysis.
- Added strategy dashboard.
- Connected Strategy Center to Data Intelligence, Digital Twin and Enterprise Decision Engine.
- Added documentation and smoke tests.

## Safety Rules

- Strategy analysis must use unified data model, enterprise knowledge, KPIs and historical data.
- Strategic scenarios must run in Digital Twin sandbox.
- Scenario analysis must not modify production data.
- High-risk strategic execution remains subject to Enterprise Decision Engine approval gates.
