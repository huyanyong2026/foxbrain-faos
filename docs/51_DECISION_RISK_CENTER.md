# 51 Decision Risk Center

## Goal

Decision and Risk Centers unify decisions and risks from all modules.

## Routes

- `/decisions`
- `/risks`
- `/timeline`

## Risk Model

`system_risks`

- Title
- Risk type
- Level
- Object type
- Evidence
- Recommended action
- Status
- Owner
- Due date

## Timeline

The global timeline combines system events and audit logs.
