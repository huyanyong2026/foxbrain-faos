# Enterprise Pack 16 - AI Strategy Center

## Goal

Pack 16 establishes the AI Strategy Center as the strategic planning layer for FoxBrain OS.
It supports long-term business planning, OKR alignment, strategic initiative evaluation,
scenario comparison and explainable recommendations.

## Core Rule

All strategy analysis must be based on:

- Unified data model
- Enterprise knowledge
- Operating KPIs
- Historical data
- Digital Twin simulation
- Enterprise Decision Engine

The Strategy Center must remain consistent with Data Intelligence, Digital Twin and the
Enterprise Decision Engine.

## API Contracts

- `/api/strategy-center/framework`
- `/api/strategy-center/okr`
- `/api/strategy-center/models`
- `/api/strategy-center/scenario-comparison`
- `/api/strategy-center/expansion-analysis`
- `/api/strategy-center/brand-product-strategy`
- `/api/strategy-center/dashboard`

## OKR Framework

Supported levels:

- Corporate OKRs
- Department OKRs
- Store OKRs
- Progress tracking
- KPI linkage

Every OKR should link to the unified KPI catalog and metrics service.

## Strategy Models

Initial model families:

- Growth strategy
- Profit strategy
- Brand and product strategy
- Expansion strategy

## Scenario Comparison

Supported strategic scenarios:

- Open a flagship store
- Adjust discount strategy
- Introduce new brands

Scenario comparison runs in the Digital Twin sandbox and must not alter production data.

## Expansion Planning

Expansion analysis evaluates:

- New store opportunities
- Relocation options
- Investment estimates
- Payback analysis
- Risk assessment

## Brand and Product Strategy

The Strategy Center analyzes:

- Brand portfolio
- Product lifecycle
- Category performance
- Promotion effectiveness
