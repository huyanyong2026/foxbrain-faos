# 50 Workspace System

## Goal

Workspace System gives every user a role-aware home for daily work.

## Routes

- `/workspace`
- `/boss`
- `/employee-workspace`

## Sections

- My tasks
- My notifications
- My documents
- My reports
- Pending approvals
- Favorite modules
- Store or department context

Boss workspace focuses on AI CEO, risks, decisions and reports.

Employee workspace focuses on tasks, mobile submissions, training, customer follow-up and asking AI.
