# VAFOX OS 5.0 Explainable, Traceable and Auditable AI

## Explainable

AI must show why it recommends something. A recommendation should explain:

- Which business signal triggered it.
- Which SAP metric or business record supports it.
- Which risks or constraints affect it.
- What is still unknown.

## Traceable

AI must show where the recommendation came from:

- SAP B1 summary or synced tables.
- Knowledge items.
- Approved memory.
- Decision history.
- Risk center.
- AI operation plan.
- Digital employee run.

## Auditable

AI must leave a record:

- Who requested or saved the recommendation.
- Which evidence was used.
- Whether approval is required.
- Whether the recommendation was reviewed.
- Whether any operation was blocked.

## Safety Boundary

High-risk operations must retain human approval. Approval does not mean uncontrolled automatic execution; execution must happen through an approved business workflow with audit records.

## SAP Rule

SAP B1 remains the system of record for business data. If SAP data is stale, missing or partially synced, AI must state the limitation and avoid strong conclusions.
