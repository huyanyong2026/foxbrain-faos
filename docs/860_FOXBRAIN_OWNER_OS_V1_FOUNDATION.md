# FoxBrain Owner OS V1 Foundation

## Positioning

FoxBrain Owner OS is the owner's enterprise second brain.

It is not ERP, not a wiki and not a general employee system. It is the System of Intelligence for `huyan.vafox.com`.

## Product Principles

- Owner OS is not ERP. Sales orders, purchase orders and inventory transactions belong to SAP.
- Owner OS is not OA. Attendance, leave, employee workflow and routine execution belong to Enterprise OS.
- Everything important becomes knowledge: archive, summary, risk, timeline, version and relationship.
- Everything important has relationships: stores, brands, suppliers, employees, assets, contracts, photos, videos, decisions and AI advice.
- AI is the default owner entry. The owner asks questions directly and receives evidence-based answers.

## SAP Principle

SAP remains the independent System of Record.

Owner OS is the System of Intelligence.

Enterprise OS is the System of Execution.

SAP never installs AI, experiments, Dify, ChatGPT or knowledge-base services. SAP writeback requires human approval.
