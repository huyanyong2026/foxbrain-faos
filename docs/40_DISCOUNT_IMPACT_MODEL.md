# 40 Discount Impact Model

## Goal

Discount Impact Model estimates whether a discount scenario still contributes profit.

## Inputs

- `cost_discount`
- `selling_discount`
- `rebate_rate`
- `sales_amount`
- `inventory_amount`
- `fixed_expense_allocation`
- `variable_expense_rate`

## Outputs

- Gross margin
- Gross profit
- Rebate amount
- Rebate adjusted gross profit
- Estimated net contribution
- Risk level

## Break-even Model

Inputs:

- `fixed_expenses`
- `gross_margin_rate`
- `target_profit`
- `sales_target`

Outputs:

- Break-even sales
- Profit at current sales
- Required sales increase
- Warning level

## Safety

The calculator is a scenario tool only. It must not replace reviewed finance results from SAP B1 or accounting records.
