# 41 HR Performance Engine

## Goal

HR Performance Engine helps managers review employee performance, training, growth and execution.

## Page

- `/hr`

## Model

`hr_performance_records`

- Employee and store
- Sales amount
- Gross profit and margin
- Completed tasks
- Customer feedback
- Content submissions
- Training completion
- Attendance status
- Manager review
- AI evaluation placeholder

## Integration

- Employee Archive
- Store Growth Engine
- Finance Profit Engine
- Task Center
- Mobile Field Operation
- Reporting Engine
- Jarvis

## Safety

AI evaluation is advisory only. Final personnel decisions require human review.
