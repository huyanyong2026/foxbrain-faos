# BA-V2.0-D Store Daily Briefing

Status: PASS

The daily briefing removes manual report filling. It summarizes Store Status, Sales Summary, Inventory Risk, Opportunity, and Today's Actions from Core sales, inventory, product, customer, task, and event facts.

## Output Contract

- `store_status`: Good or Attention.
- `sales_summary`: 30-day sales, unit trend, top brand, product trends, and opportunity.
- `inventory_risk`: low stock or slow inventory risk.
- `opportunity`: highest sales growth product or brand.
- `todays_actions`: prioritized execution guidance.

Example: Nanshan status is Good, opportunity is hiking footwear growth, risk is Osprey inventory low, actions are transfer/replenishment and improved display.
