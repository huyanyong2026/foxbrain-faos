# FoxBrain Business Activation V2.0-B CEO Intelligence Upgrade

Status: PASS

This document supports the BA-V2.0-B upgrade of Huyan from CEO dashboard to CEO AI Strategic Assistant.

## Architecture Principle

SAP B1 remains the business truth. The Core Enterprise Data Layer remains the enterprise data source. AI modules provide analysis, prediction, recommendation, simulation, and decision memory without modifying SAP logic or creating a duplicate business database.

## Implemented Scope

- CEO Daily Executive Briefing: what happened, why it happened, and what to do next.
- Enterprise Risk Prediction: risk, level, probability, reason, and recommendation.
- Opportunity Radar: opportunity, reason, and recommended action.
- CEO Decision Center: problem, analysis, options, recommendation, and expected result.
- Business Simulation: sales, margin, inventory, risk, and ROI expectations.
- CEO Memory: decision learning structure connected to existing AI memory records.
- Huyan Command Center V2: enterprise health score and CEO assistant entry.
- AI Agent Integration: CEO, Supply Chain, Finance, Store, and Growth agent orchestration model.
- Security: RBAC, ABAC data scopes, and audit log alignment.

## Acceptance

BA-V2.0-B acceptance criteria are marked PASS for briefing, risk prediction, opportunity radar, decision center, simulation, CEO memory, Huyan command center, security, and deployment readiness.
