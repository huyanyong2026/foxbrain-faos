# VAFOX Design Language

Version: Genesis-Construction-002  
Brand: VAFOX / 火狐狸  
Product: VAFOX Outdoor LIFE  
Experience theme: Welcome Home  
Powered by: FoxBrain Intelligence Engine.

## 1. Brand Meaning

VAFOX Outdoor LIFE is a digital home for the enterprise and its ecosystem. The experience should feel alive, warm, capable, and trustworthy.

The brand should communicate:

- Outdoor vitality.
- Fox-like intelligence and agility.
- Human warmth.
- Enterprise trust.
- AI companionship.

## 2. Experience Promise

Welcome Home means:

- The system knows who the user is.
- The system understands the user's relationship to VAFOX.
- The system explains what matters today.
- The system helps the user move with confidence.
- The system protects the enterprise foundation.

## 3. Voice and Tone

Voice principles:

- Clear.
- Calm.
- Direct.
- Supportive.
- Evidence-aware.
- Mission-oriented.

Avoid:

- Cold ERP language.
- Overly technical AI jargon.
- Dashboard-heavy language.
- Unverified certainty.
- Manipulative gamification.

Preferred phrasing examples:

- “Welcome home.”
- “Here is what matters today.”
- “I can help you prepare the next step.”
- “This answer uses recent Core data.”
- “This action needs approval before execution.”

## 4. Visual Principles

Visual direction:

- Mobile-first calm layout.
- Warm brand accents inspired by 火狐狸.
- Clear cards for Today, Mission, Risk, and Decision.
- Strong hierarchy over dense grids.
- Progressive detail.
- Evidence and freshness visible when it matters.

The design should not imitate traditional ERP portals or BI dashboards.

## 5. Component Language

Foundation components:

- Home Header: identity, role, relationship, session confidence.
- Today Card: prioritized short summary.
- Mission Card: next action and status.
- AI Companion Entry: natural-language prompt.
- Evidence Drawer: source, freshness, confidence, lineage.
- Decision Card: decision, options, impact, approval path.
- Growth Card: learning and capability progress.

## 6. AI Presentation Rules

AI should appear as a companion embedded in the Home, not as a separate tool picker.

AI outputs should distinguish:

- Facts from Core.
- Inferences from FoxBrain.
- Recommendations from Mission Engine.
- Actions requiring workflow or approval.

AI must respect permission, freshness, and governance boundaries.

## 7. Ecosystem Consistency

Future Customer Home, Supplier Home, Brand Home, Club Home, and Community Home should reuse the same design language:

```text
Identity → Today → Mission / Need → AI Help → Learning / Relationship Growth
```

Each Home may adapt vocabulary, but should still feel like VAFOX Outdoor LIFE.
