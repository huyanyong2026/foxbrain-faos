# VAFOX Foundation V2.0 Operations

## Daily Operating Checks

- Gateway authentication, routing, permissions, rate limits, and audit logs.
- Huyan CEO dashboard, daily briefing, risk radar, and decision center.
- AI agent registry, workspace, knowledge connection, and memory review.
- Core master data, events, business APIs, data quality, source tracking, and audit metadata.

## CEO Daily Briefing

The daily briefing must include:

- What happened.
- Why it happened.
- Recommended actions.

## Decision Center

Each AI recommendation should show:

- Problem.
- Reason.
- Recommendation.
- Action.

## Human Control

AI suggestions and memory promotion require human approval before becoming operational actions or institutional knowledge.
