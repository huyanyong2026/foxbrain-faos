# AI Workforce V2 Guide — Digital Employee Platform

## Purpose
AI Workforce OS V2 turns the AI portal into a governed digital employee platform.

## My AI Team
The V2 surface presents CEO Agent, Supply Chain Agent, Store Agent, Supplier Agent, Customer Agent, Growth Agent, Finance Agent, and HR Agent.

## Agent Center
Each agent exposes purpose, status, permission, knowledge source, memory, and tasks.

## AI Task Center
Tracks running tasks, completed tasks, and pending approvals.

## Knowledge Center
Connects documents, products, brands, training, and business knowledge.

## Memory Center
Stores business experience, user feedback, and decision history.

## Route
Open `/ai-workforce-v2` or `/digital-employee` after login.
