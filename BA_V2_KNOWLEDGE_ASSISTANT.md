# BA V2.0-C Knowledge Assistant

## Goal
Create company knowledge AI connected to the approved Knowledge Center.

## Content Scope
Company Policy, Product Knowledge, Brand Knowledge, Training Material, and Operation Procedure.

## Retrieval Rule
Answers must return approved company knowledge with source reference and update time. Draft knowledge remains review-gated before publication.

## Example
Question: `折扣政策是什么？`

AI returns the approved discount policy, evidence, and knowledge source reference.
