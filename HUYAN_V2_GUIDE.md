# Huyan V2 Guide — CEO Operating System V2

## Purpose
Huyan V2 is the CEO Command Center for enterprise health, daily briefing, risk radar, opportunity radar, and decision workflows.

## Home Modules
- Enterprise Health Dashboard: health score, sales, margin, inventory, supply chain, risk, and AI recommendation.
- CEO Daily Briefing: what happened, why it happened, and what should happen next.
- Risk Radar: sales, inventory, supply, store, and operation risk.
- Opportunity Radar: growth, product, and market opportunity discovery.
- Decision Center: Problem → Reason → Recommendation → Action.

## Guardrails
Huyan V2 uses existing business data and AI foundations. Human approval remains required for execution-sensitive decisions.

## Route
Open `/huyan-v2` or `/ceo-os-v2` after login.
