# BA-V2.0-D Store AI Assistant

Status: PASS

Store employees ask natural-language questions without manually choosing agents. The AI Router selects Store Agent, Supply Agent, Commerce Agent, Customer Agent, and Growth Agent based on intent.

## Supported Questions

- 今天南山店重点关注什么？
- 哪些商品需要重点推荐？
- 库存风险是什么？
- Scarpa和LOWA区别？

## Response Sources

Responses combine Core product, sales, inventory, customer, task, and event facts with Store AI recommendations. WeCom employee access is routed through the employee assistant and permission model.
